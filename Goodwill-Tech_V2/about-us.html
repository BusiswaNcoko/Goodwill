About us

Goodwill Tech is a tech company founded in 2021 by young developers
Our aim is to have every South African child computer literate by the time they enter university.

What we do

We provide a platform for individuals and corperates to donate computer hardware.
It may be recycled hardware or brand new.
We work both with the schools and department of basic education for accurate data of the schools in need.
